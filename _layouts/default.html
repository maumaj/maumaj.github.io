Martha Graham was an American dancer of the 20th century, known for her contributions to the artistic vocabulary of modern dance. Her work drew on the influence of German choreographer Harold Kreutzberg, an innovator in bringing theatrical features into a dance setting. In addition, she emphasized human emotion and its effect on the physical body through the practice of "contraction" and "release."
